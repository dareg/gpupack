Compilation scripts in details

In this chapter we shall review from top to bottom a compilation script from top to bottom and explain how to tune parameters to get the best of it.

Note that all the options described below may not appear in all scripts : it depends whether the script is specific for building precompilers or not, whether the user is ''administrator'' or not, and whether a batch scheduler is used or not.
